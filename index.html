About Us

ADNOX HEALTHCARE is dedicated and committed to serve the Healthcare community by providing innovative, high quality equipments, surgical products, consumables & services from the world’s leading manufacturers so that our customers can deliver their best care. Our home care product range consists of all popular brands to ensure the best choice for every individual. We also have a laboratory and pharmacy to cater to the public.

We have partnered with major medical equipment manufacturers to provide customer-oriented reliable services and focus on implementing technologies that best suit each organization’s needs, while offering the widest selection of medical supplies and medical equipment products. Whether you are an individual, a medical professional, or a medical establishment, ADNOX Healthcare is the source for all your healthcare product needs.
